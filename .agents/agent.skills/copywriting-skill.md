## 1. Core Copywriting Philosophy
[cite_start]The agent must adhere to the principle that people buy based on emotion and justify with logic[cite: 963].
* [cite_start]**Emotion First, Logic Second**: Lead with the emotional payoff (e.g., "Peace of mind for your family") before providing technical justifications like warranties or certifications[cite: 963].
* [cite_start]**The "You" Focus**: Use "you" and "your" more frequently than "we" or "our" to keep the focus on the client’s home and comfort[cite: 1119, 1120].
* [cite_start]**Active Voice Only**: Write in the active voice (e.g., "We repair your roof") to create direct accountability[cite: 1118].
* [cite_start]**Clarity Over Cleverness**: Favor direct, punchy headlines over vague metaphors to avoid confusing users in high-stress situations[cite: 972].

## 2. Cialdini’s Principles of Persuasion
[cite_start]Integrate these triggers into the site’s structural narrative[cite: 964, 965]:
* [cite_start]**Social Proof**: Curate and place localized reviews (e.g., "your neighbors in Silver Lake") near Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons[cite: 968, 1109].
* [cite_start]**Authority**: Conspicuously display license numbers, GAF Master Elite badges, or NATE certifications in a "Trust Bar"[cite: 968, 985].
* [cite_start]**Reciprocity**: Offer a "Home Energy Assessment" or "Maintenance Checklist" without immediate obligation[cite: 968].
* [cite_start]**Liking**: Humanize the brand with "Meet the Team" sections and multi-generational family-owned narratives[cite: 968, 995].
* [cite_start]**Scarcity**: Use emergency-oriented copy like "Only 3 appointments left this week" or "Emergency 24/7 Service"[cite: 968].
* [cite_start]**Consistency**: Start contact forms with low-friction questions (e.g., "What is your zip code?") to secure micro-commitments[cite: 968, 1100].

## 4. Page-Specific Conversion Logic
* [cite_start]**Hero Section ("The 4-Second Pitch")**: Headlines must be localized and problem-specific (e.g., "Same-Day AC Repair in Pasadena") rather than generic[cite: 971, 976].
* [cite_start]**Service Solutions**: Structure services as solutions to painful problems (e.g., "Lower your energy bills by 25%") rather than a list of tools[cite: 1013, 1015].
* [cite_start]**About & Safety**: Frame safety as a "cultural pillar" (e.g., "Our people are our most valuable tool") and emphasize local heritage to communicate stability[cite: 1008, 1042].
